# Ideation Phase

## Problem Statement
Define the problem you are trying to solve. The problem statement should be clear and concise, articulating the needs and goals influencing your project.

## Empathy Map
Create an empathy map to understand the users. This could include aspects like what users think, feel, say, and do regarding the problem at hand.

## Brainstorming Sessions
Describe the brainstorming sessions conducted.
- Session 1: Date - Participants - Ideas Generated
- Session 2: Date - Participants - Ideas Generated

## Final Concept Selection
Outline the final concept chosen to move forward with in development. Include criteria used for the selection and why this concept was prioritized over others.
